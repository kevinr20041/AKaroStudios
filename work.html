Featured project

Wedding websites and invitations, crafted together.

A live, working template couples can see today

A wedding website is a genuinely great idea.

Every couple sends out invites and then fields the same ten questions on repeat: where, when, what to wear, can the kids come, where should we stay. A dedicated wedding site answers all of it in one link, feels personal from the moment it opens, and doubles as a keepsake of the whole day afterwards. We built a full working demo, under our Website Creation service, to show couples exactly what's possible before they book.

  • A live countdown timer to the big day
  • A virtual guestbook for messages before and after the wedding
  • An RSVP link built straight into the couple's email invites
  • Photos of the couple and their story
  • Full day details: ceremony & reception venues, timings, dress code, travel & accommodation
  • Gift registry & honeymoon fund links
  • An add-to-calendar button so the date lands straight in every guest's phone
  • A custom domain, like niamhandconor.com, instead of a generic link

That list is just a starting point — whatever idea a couple brings us, we can design and build it into their site.
