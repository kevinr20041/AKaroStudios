2024 the year we merged three disciplines into one studio

Why we merged three disciplines into one studio.

We started as three separate specialists: one building websites, one running email programmes, one managing local search. Every project hit the same wall. The web team shipped a beautiful site, then nobody sent an email from it. The email team wrote a brilliant welcome flow, then it linked back to a homepage from 2016. The local search specialist fixed a Google Business Profile, then it pointed to a broken contact form.

So we merged. AKaro Studios is one senior team across all three disciplines, which means one strategist can see your whole funnel: the search, the click, the site, the signup, and the email that brings someone back.
