What Email Marketing includes.

Every subject line, flow and report, planned, written and built in-house.

Most brands treat email as an afterthought: a discount blast when sales are slow. We treat it as owned media — the one channel where you control the audience, the message and the timing completely. That means a real calendar, flows that work while you sleep, and copy and design that finally look as good as your product.

Every account gets a single strategist who knows your catalogue, your list and your numbers, backed by a copywriter and designer who learn your tone from week one.

What's included

  • Strategy & campaign planning, tied to your product calendar
  • Flows & automation: welcome, cart, browse and win-back
  • List growth & segmentation, so every send feels personal
  • Copy & design, on-brand from subject line to footer
  • Reporting & optimisation, reviewed with you monthly
