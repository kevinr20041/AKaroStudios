Why it matters

Why your listing matters as much as your site.

01

"Near me" searches convert fast

Most local searches end in a call, a visit or a direction request within 24 hours. Your profile is the moment of decision.

02

It often outranks your own website

Google frequently shows your Business Profile above your website in local search. If it's thin, that's the first thing customers see.

03

Reviews and activity drive ranking

Fresh posts, photos and review responses directly influence how Google ranks you locally, not just how customers perceive you.
